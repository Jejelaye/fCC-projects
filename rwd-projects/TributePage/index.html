Quick facts about Donald J. Trump

  • Born in 1946
  • Graduated in 1968 from Wharton School of Business at the University of Pennsylvania
  • Moved to Manhattan in the 1970s
  • Declared bankruptcy in 1991
  • Owned part of Miss Universe from 1996 to 2015
  • Became executive producer of NBC's "The Apprentice"
  • Annouced intention to run for president under the Republican Umbrella in 2015
  • Elected president in November 2016
  • Took office on Jan 20, 2017
